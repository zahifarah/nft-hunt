What is it NFT Hunt?

NFT Hunt is a gamified marketplace in which users can track down NFTs anywhere in the world.

Whether it's a new song, an animated renaissance style painting or a unique piece of armor for that indie game you're playing; access a new layer of interaction between artists and collectors at the touch of your fingertips.

Proud participants of the Chainlink Hackathon Spring 2021.

Artists

Place your digital artwork anywhere in the world and fully customize the parameters around them.

Create auctions, hide clues, add discovery rewards, loyalty points, timers, smart royalties and more! NFT Hunt was conceived to empower artists with control over both the distribution of their work, and the conditions of the distribution. Bring it back to the artists.

Collectors

Connect with your favorite artists in a whole new way.

Collect unique digital assets, display them anywhere in the world, trade them, sell them outright, bundle them up, exchange them for a service or benefit from exclusive access from them. One of the main philosophies of NFT Hunt is to broaden the scope of interaction between artists and their fans and reward it.
