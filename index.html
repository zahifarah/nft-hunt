What is it NFT Hunt?

NFT Hunt is a gamified marketplace in which users can track down NFTs anywhere in the world, whether it's a new song, an animated renaissance style painting or a unique piece of armor for that indy game you're playing. Access a new layer of interaction with your favorite artists at the touch of your fingertips.

Artists

Place your digital art work anywhere in the world and fully customize the parameters around them. Create auctions, hide clues, add discovery rewards, loyalty points, timers, smart royalties and more!

Collectors

Connect and interact with your favorite artists in a whole new way. Collect unique digital assets, display them anywhere in the world, trade them, sell them outright, bundle them up, exchange them for a service or benefit from exclusive access from them.
